## Base ledger

The base ledger of Namada includes a [consensus mechanism](./base-ledger/consensus.md) and a validity predicate-based [execution system](./base-ledger/execution.md). In addition to describing the exuction model and consensus, this section  also documents Namada's [replay protection system](./base-ledger/replay-protection.md), and [block space allocator](./base-ledger/block-space-allocator.md).
